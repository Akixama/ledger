No. 002 — The problem

      
Most agents guess. 
Guessing breaks pipelines.

      

        

          GENERIC AGENT — OUTPUT
          REJECTED
        


-- assumes a column that was renamed 6 months ago
SELECT customer_ltv_score
FROM   marts.customer_summary
WHERE  region = 'unknown'  -- guessed default

        
No schema check. No lineage. No owner pinged. Fails in CI, or worse, in production.

      

      
Without reliable knowledge of schemas, lineage, ownership, and governance, agents hallucinate or stall on work a data engineer could finish in minutes. Ledger closes that gap.
